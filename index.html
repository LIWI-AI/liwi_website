About Liwi

Liwi uses advanced AI to personalize English education, adapting to each learner's pace and style. Research shows AI tutors accelerate language learning by 3x through instant feedback and adaptive content.

Our Story

Founded by a simple accountant who struggled to learn English himself, Liwi was born from personal experience with language barriers. Understanding the challenges faced by millions of learners, we created an AI-powered solution that makes English accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Make 500 million English learners from underserved regions fluent in English, breaking down language barriers worldwide. We believe everyone deserves the opportunity to communicate confidently in English.

Our Vision

Learn smarter. Learn faster. Learn with AI. We envision a world where advanced technology makes quality English education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or location.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Liwi and how our AI-powered English tutoring works

How do I practice with LIWI?

Download LIWI app, complete a quick assessment, then practice daily with our AI tutor. Complete lessons, earn gems, and track your confidence improvement. Just 10-15 minutes daily is enough for great results.

How does the free trial work?

Get 3 days of full premium access completely free. No credit card required. Try all features including AI tutor and advanced lessons. Cancel anytime within 3 days for zero charges.

How do I cancel my free trial?

Go to Profile → Settings → Cancel Trial in the app. Or cancel through your phone's subscription settings. You can also email info@myliwi.com. No charges if you cancel within 3 days.

Can I sign up multiple times with different numbers?

No, only one free trial per device. You cannot use multiple phone numbers on the same device for additional trials. Each family member can get their own trial on separate devices.

Can I become fluent in English in 30 days?

LIWI can make you 40-60% more confident in 30 days and help you handle daily conversations. Full fluency takes 3-6 months with consistent practice. Practice 10-20 minutes daily for best results.
