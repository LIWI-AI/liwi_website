  • No Third-Party Sales: Your data is never sold to advertisers or unrelated parties.

We safeguard your information, especially for young users:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest (SSL/TLS, AES-256).

  • Access & Correction: View or update information via your account or by contacting us.
  • Deletion: Request data deletion (subject to legal requirements).

  • Portability: Request a data copy in a structured format.

Email myliwiai@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

LIWI welcomes users aged 10 and up, with special care for young learners:

  • Parental Consent: For users under 18, we require verifiable parental consent (e.g., email verification) to collect personal data.
  • Limited Data: We collect minimal data from children (e.g., name, age, practice inputs) to ensure safe, age-appropriate learning.
