1. Introduction

Liwi Learning Solutions Pvt.Ltd ("LIWI," "Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, store, and protect your personal information when you use the LIWI AI-powered English tutoring application and related services.

Important: This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), Information Technology Act, 2000, and other applicable Indian data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data Categories

We collect the following types of personal data:

2.1.1 Account and Profile Information
  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Language preferences
  • Learning goals and objectives
  • Age range (for appropriate content delivery)
2.1.2 Voice and Audio Data
  • Voice recordings during speaking exercises
  • Speech patterns and pronunciation data
  • Audio responses to learning prompts
  • Conversation practice recordings
  • Pronunciation assessment data
2.1.3 Learning and Performance Data
  • Learning progress and achievements
  • Test scores and assessment results
  • Time spent on different exercises
  • Difficulty levels and preferences
  • Learning patterns and behavior
  • Areas of improvement and strengths
2.1.4 Technical and Usage Data
  • Device information (type, model, operating system)
  • IP address and location data
  • App usage statistics and analytics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Session duration and frequency

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Multi-factor authentication for admin access

7. Your Rights Under DPDP Act

As a data principal under the DPDP Act, you have the following rights:

7.1 Right to Information

You have the right to know what personal data we collect, how it's used, and who it's shared with.

7.2 Right of Access

You can request a copy of your personal data we hold. We will provide this within 30 days of your request.

7.3 Right to Correction

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data. We will update your information promptly.

7.4 Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data. Note that some data may be retained for legal compliance.

7.5 Right to Data Portability

You can export your learning data and progress in a commonly used format.

7.6 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@myliwi.com or through the app settings. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

8.1 Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Until account deletion + 30 days for backup purposes
  • Voice Recordings: 30 days unless saved by user for progress tracking
  • Learning Analytics: 2 years for service improvement and research

  • Payment Data: As required by financial regulations (up to 7 years)

8.2 Automatic Deletion

We automatically delete data when retention periods expire, unless legal obligations require longer retention.
